Welcome to the Unscramble! To start, click the, "Start Game" button,You'll then be prompted to enter your name to personalize the game and a scrambled word will appear on the screen. Your goal is to guess the correct word from the scrambled letters within 3 attempts. Points are awarded based on how quickly you guess: 15 points for a correct guess on the first attempt, 10 points on the second attempt, and 5 points on the third attempt. If you don't guess correctly within 3 attempts, you can click the "New Word" button to try another scrambled word with a fresh set of attempts. After the second attempt, a helpful hint will appear to assist you in solving the word. If you want to reset the game at any time, simply click the "Reset Game" button, which will clear your score and start you over. Challenge yourself to guess the words with as few attempts as possible to earn the highest score! Enjoy the game and have fun unscrambling!
